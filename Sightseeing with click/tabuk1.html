Tabuk

Places you should visit in Tabuk :

-Hejaz railway Museum :

Is one of the best preserved in Saudi Arabia.
There are 13 recently refurbished buildings and these include a workshop, a handicrafts centre, and a building that houses a locomotive, a freight car and several Ottoman relics worth viewing.
The site is also going to be the home of Tabuk's new state-of-the-art city museum.



-Boulevard:

It is one of the most distinguished projects, as it was designed according to the latest and finest international designs, and there are the most famous and finest cafes and restaurants.



-TABUK PARK mall:

Tabuk Park is one of the important projects that added to the region the idea of ​​shopping and entertainment at new and innovative levels, and it is the largest commercial center in the north of the Kingdom.



-Prince Fahd bin Sultan Park :

You can find a lot of deferent things to do and visit there like:
Sports- Restaurants and Cafes - Walking and running track-Entertainment and educational programs.
