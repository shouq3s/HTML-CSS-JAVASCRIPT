-Have you heard of Saudi Titanic ??

In Tabuk particularly in Haql, the wreck of the semi-sunken "Georgios G" is located in the clear shore waters with a backdrop of high mountains, forming a unique sight and a stunning backdrop.
Because of its fame with local legends, this ship, known as the "Saudi Titanic", arouses the curiosity of visitors .
Locals advise people who want to visit to "bring your senses of adventure, and be prepared to surprise", not only for the impressive wreck, but also for the full adventures.
