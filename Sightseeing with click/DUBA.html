Places you should visit in DUBĀ :

-Corniche Duba :

The new Corniche is one of the most beautiful tourist places in Duba, where fresh air, crystal clear waters and new entertainment places are located. It is the perfect and best place to spend holidays with family members or friends.

In the marina, you can do many adventures there all you have to do is meet one of the locals and they will tell you what to do!.
