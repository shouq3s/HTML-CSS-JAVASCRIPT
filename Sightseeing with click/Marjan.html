about Marjan Island




For perfect picnic spots away from the hustle and bustle of the city, do consider a trip to Dammam’s Marjan Island. Built in 1991, Marjan Island is a man-made archipelago consisting of four coral-shaped isles with several hotel resorts and beaches. It is a great place to take a relaxing stroll and enjoy the peace and quiet.


Marjan is also considered to be a popular fishing spot as well as a wonderful place to enjoy a barbeque with the family while appreciating the distinctive views of Dammam at night.




Facilities

This island is home to King Abdullah Park, which hosts many celebrations, festivals, bazaars and national events. There is a main entrance as well as two towers on both sides. There are also four pitches and courts to play football, volleyball and basketball, as well as dedicated areas to walk, jog and cycle.
